For example, when visiting our App, we log IP address, device information, browser information, operating system information and information about your use of and actions on our App. While you use the Services, we also collect information related to how you interact with us or how you wish to interact with us. Making a verifiable consumer request does not require you to create an account with us. If you make a request, we have 45 days to respond to you.
